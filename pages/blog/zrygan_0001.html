The Gist of Complexity

Zhean Ganituen, April 9, 2025
Keywords: Algorithms, Complexity Theory
Complexity Theory, one of the most important fields of computer science if you're an aspiring software engineer, is also one of the most theoretical undergraduate computer science classes you may encounter. As the crux of all theoretical computer science lies on this very field. What is complexity, what are problems, what are algorithms, are all problems solvable?
Honestly, isn't that cool, SMB is a problem? But, what's cooler is that SMB is "that" problem.
Questions like "What is the shortest path of graph \(G\)?", "Determine the 3rd element of the sorted array \(A\).", and even games like Super Mario Brothers (Demaine) are the central questions of complexity. While, the central goal of complexity theory is to determine the classes of these problems, and the relationship of these problems to each other.
